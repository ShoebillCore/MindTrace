Privacy Policy

MindTraceReader  ·  Last updated: May 2025

Overview

MindTraceReader is a Chrome extension that extracts article content from webpages and provides an AI-assisted reading workspace. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, what it stores, and what it never does.

Data we collect

We collect no data. MindTraceReader has no servers, no analytics, and no telemetry. Nothing you read, highlight, or type is ever transmitted to us.

What the extension accesses

  • Page content — when you click the toolbar icon, the extension reads the current page's text to extract the article. This happens entirely in your browser and is never sent to any MindTraceReader server.
  • API keys you enter — keys are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. They are sent exclusively and directly to the AI provider you choose (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or Deepseek) when you use the chat feature. We never see or receive your API keys.
  • Highlights and notes — stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device. They never leave your browser.
  • Reader preferences — font size, theme, and similar settings are stored locally in chrome.storage.local.

Third-party AI providers

When you use the chat feature, article text and your messages are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider whose API key you configured. Please review the privacy policy of your chosen provider:

  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • OpenAI
  • Google (Gemini)
  • Deepseek

Permissions explained

  • storage — to save your highlights, notes, API keys, and preferences locally.
  • tabs — to open the workspace tab when you click the extension icon.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — to read page content on any site you choose to open in MindTraceReader. The extension only activates when you explicitly click the toolbar icon.

Children's privacy

MindTraceReader does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy
